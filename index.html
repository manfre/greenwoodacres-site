Greenwood Acres Neighbors Helping Neighbors

We're an awesome little neighborbood in Cary, NC that believe it's important to interact with the people that live around us and to be neighborly.

From time to time we have social events in the neighborhood. Join the mailing list to find out about these activities.

We are not an HOA. There are no dues or fees.

Mailing List

We've created a mailing list for Greenwood Acres residents. Access to the list is restricted to community residents.

To request access to the list, send an email to Join the mailing list and include your name, address, and phone number.
